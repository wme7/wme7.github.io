Taught a 16-hour online certification program: 8 hours on Beamer presentations and 8 hours on LaTeX document management for thesis writing. Reference: Hugo A. Carrillo.

In 2021, I was invited by Prof. Hugo A. Carrillo to contribute to a short course to LaTeX for the students of the Tecnológico Nacional de México. In this opportunity, I was in charge of introducing the Beamer package to build academic presentations and posters.

The base tutorial material I contributed to the course can be found in the following github repositories: 
1. [LaTeX beamer](https://github.com/wme7/LaTeX_beamer).
2. [LaTeX beamer poster](https://github.com/wme7/LaTeX_beamer_poster)
