Taught a 32-hour online internship in Kinematics and Ordinary Differential Equations. Reference: Miguel Montalvo and Erick Urquilla.

![wheel_damped_motion]({{ base_path }}/files/figures/bumpy_road/bumpy_road.gif)

On June 2025, I collaborated to the a Inspira STEM internship project to enhance the students' skills in kinematics and ordinary differential equations. This internship lasted two months: from June 1st to July 31st. Which culminated in a symposium where the students presented their projects.

This was a two month-long internship that consisted of two parts:
1. A 8-hour introduction to coding and linear algebra with Python (NumPy, SymPy and SciPy).
2. A 24-hour introduction to ordinary differential equations with applications to kinematics.
